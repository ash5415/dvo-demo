 CI/CD :

    Jenkins:
          --> Jenkins is a open source software.
          --> A continuous integration server which manages and control process such as plan, code, build, test
                deploy and operate in monitor in Devops environment.
    Benefits of Jenkins:
                   --> open source.
                   --> good support.
                   --> good community support.
                   --> fast and reliable.
                   --> good os support.
                   --> script builds.
                   --> provide advanced security. 
                   --> upgrades are easily available.
                   --> lightweight container support.
                   --> distributed team management.
